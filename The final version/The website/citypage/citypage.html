About SentraGrid :

SentraGrid is a visionary smart city nestled on the banks of the Nile, officially launched in 2025. Designed for seamless living, it combines solar-powered rooftops, autonomous-shuttle networks, and real-time IoT sensors that monitor traffic, air quality, and energy use. Our mission is to blend cutting-edge technology with sustainable design, creating a safe, efficient, and green urban environment where every resident can thrive.
